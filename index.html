Considering women and children the integral part of our society, Sudaksh intend to spread the knowledge of not using plastic bags and replacing these with cloth bags is the best they can do at their levels to save our mother earth from unwanted plastic.

Awareness Programme

Sudaksh as a team targets school going children, take up schools' visits , interacting with the students, empowering them to spead information among their friends and family members on cleanliness and waste segregation, explaining their important role in waste management.

Sudaksh had conducted a public awareness programme in the form of a small play addressing how waste segregation plays an essential role in waste management and regarding harmful impacts that plastic put to our planet.
