About Us

The idea to start off Sudaksh, De nayi Pehchan (Non Government Organisation) by five community members - Mohit Bansal, Harish Gupta, Amit Kumar, Ram Narayan Sharma and Rajesh Sherawat.
The ideology behind to start the NGO to give overall development to underprivileged children, special children, financially backward people and also the old - age people.
At the same time, NGO focused on environmental issues as well such as environmental health, waste segregation and insisting people to follow the same.
Sudaksh organised many programmes like distributing books, food, clothes and medicines, organizing free classes, health check ups and did street plays on various social issues to aware the society.

Education


The organisation has addressed several issues such as child rights, importance of sex education, menstrual hygiene, sex related taboos prevailing in our society, need for gender sensitisation. Sudakh has conducted no. awareness programmes in various schools of Uttarpradesh, focussing on menstrual hygiene whereby it has mentioned some facts that providing education about menstrual hygiene to women and girls will make them feel more comfortable and also educating males about menstruation will make it a more comfortable topic to discuss. Sudaksh has conducted several sessions at schools in rural areas of Uttarpradesh to make children understand what child abuse is, what sex education is, how it impacts children and how parents and schools can be urged to make sex education a regular class in schools. The organisation has worked over facilitating a comprehensive and common understanding about gender equality and making people aware about the importance of it for the development of Society. It focuses on the fact that Gender equality and women’s empowerment can only be brought about by changing the mind-set of people and breaking the societal norms to involve men in the process.


Enviornment


Air pollution, water pollution, garbage domestically prohibited goods and pollution of the natural environment are all major challenges for India. Sudaksh has initiated it's drive for this and people who are part of this impart environmental education and education through camps for teachers and students and conduct surveys on environment in villages of Uttarpradesh and Madhya Pradesh; they have made handmade dustbins in a locality of Madhya Pradesh to spread awareness on importance of cleanliness, had distributed cloth bags to people living in those villages and also they've conducted awareness programmes on the infectious diseases caused from unclean surroundings. Public interaction has played an integral role every time.The NGO has addressed the importance of waste segregation and making them know about the difference that exists between the two(green and blue) dustbins and how people could practice waste segregation at their own level. The NGO has also given its contribution in parks restoration and also promoted public to participate.


Water


while water is a sustainable resource, it's in the meantime a limited resource. Water is essential forever for life. Sudaksh de nayi Pehchaan has undertaken considerable grassroot level work towards water conservation and management in both rural and urban scenario. It has conducted several awareness programs in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Delhi. Mass awareness is being spread by the organisation through various nukkad natak and awareness drives on water conservation and management with the active involvement of the public. Here the public interaction plays the vital role as to know about their current problems they're facing related to water depletion. As with all countries with excess water consumption depletes the overall water table. Rain water harvesting could be one solution for water collection. Collected water can be immediately used for agriculture, and with improved filtration practices to reduce water-borne pathogens, also quickly available for human consumption. The organization time to time carries out research and studies on the existence of the historical water bodies. The same time, the water bodies which have been enroached are saved through raising awareness amongst the local community.


Health


In India, some of the best healthcare in the world is delivered through the private sector. Lack of hospitals, missing doctors, ill-equipped health professionals, and paucity of funds have dogged the Indian health sector for decades. Each year nearly 40 million Indians are thrown into poverty because of out of pocket health spending. Sudaksh has undertaken its initiative for getting these disparities removed. It has conducted some healthcare activities and public awareness programmes in rural areas of Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh; in these programmes the free medicines, face masks were distributed among the people living there. Awareness programmes have been conducted to present some facts like diarrheal diseases are the primary causes of early childhood mortality, these diseases can be attributed to poor sanitation and inadequate safe drinking water. India has the world's highest incidence of rabies.The primary focus of the organisation is to fulfil the basic health and social needs of underprivileged sections of the society like poor, women, elderly, children etc. Sudaksh is continuously scouting the people living here and even providing them with the basic healthcare treatments and medicines.
