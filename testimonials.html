Prakrat


Hi this is Prakrat Gupta . I was involved in the internship project of Swachta Sarvekshan 2020 with Sudaksh organisation. My over all experience working with Municipal Corporation of Malhargarh was fantastic. We were involved in a number of activities right from awareness drives campaigns on a variety of issues , remodelling public behaviour through plastic rallies , best out of wastes etc. It gave us an insight into dynamics of local level policy implementation and how the masses do collaborate with administration in keeping their surroundings clean. Food, travel and accommodation was all upto the mark and it gave us an opportunity of collaborating with civic agencies. But surely this made me realise that civil society involvement with government can strengthen the roots of local self government in our country. Thanks Sudaksh for this wonderful opportunity !!!
