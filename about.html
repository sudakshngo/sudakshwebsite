About Sudaksh

The ideology behind to start the NGO to give overall development to underprivileged children, special children, financially backward people and also the old - age people.

At the same time, NGO focused on environmental issues as well such as environmental health, waste segregation and insisting people to follow the same.
Sudaksh organised many programmes like distributing books, food, clothes and medicines, organizing free classes, health check ups and did street plays on various social issues to aware the society.

Go Green

Habits we develop as children last a lifetime (unless of course one makes significant effort to change them). For example, if you made it a habit to read the newspaper every morning then without a doubt you will carry this habit to your deathbed. Likewise, if you developed an obnoxious habit as a child, it will take years to get rid of it. Now, how does a child develop a habit? Usually, children take up habits from elders or their role models because they look up to them. That's why as an elder it's necessary to inculcate habits that a younger one can adopt from you. How about we start with segregation of waste? Or use of cloth bags instead of plastic. We can impact lives. Let's start!

Responsible Citizen

"अलग करो अलग करो; हरे नीले में फ़र्क करो" "कपङे के थैले को उपयोग में लाएं; पन्नी को जङ से मिटाऐ" "साथी रे, हाथ से हाथ मिलाना; गंदगी को दूर है भगाना"

These are the texts from the displayed banners raising awareness against plastic. These banners were the underrated mode of communication with our audience. A lot of people joined to watch the nukkad natak performances, in the first place, due to these banners. These were the necessary visual representations which helped the teams accumulate large crowds wherever they went. Hence, every little effort in the strife against plastic counts.

Street Play

These pictures are from after the street play performances in Ratlam. Our team members approached children, foremostly, because they are the future of our nation and their facts and beliefs have to be set right. Apart from children, a team member can be seen keeping a biker on hold. The passerby has the cloth bag in his hand and he's closely examining it. Others around him are also curiously taking glances. The only purpose of our team for doing this was to spread awareness about single use plastic. Please use cloth bags, everyone. Save the Earth.
